Privacy Policy for Google Form Prefill Link Generator

Effective Date: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Google Form Prefill Link Generator" Chrome extension operates regarding user data and privacy.

Data Collection

Important: The Google Form Prefill Link Generator extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal user data.

All operations, including the analysis of Google Form fields and the generation of pre-filled URLs, are performed locally within your browser. No data is sent to any external servers, third parties, or stored permanently.

How the Extension Works

  • Local Processing: All form analysis happens locally in your browser
  • No External Requests: No network requests are made to external servers
  • Temporary Storage: Form data is only temporarily stored for multi-page forms and is cleared when the extension is closed
  • No Tracking: No user behavior, browsing history, or personal information is tracked

Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions solely for its intended functionality:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the current tab's URL and content only when the user explicitly clicks the extension icon. This is necessary to identify Google Form pages and extract form data.
  • scripting: Enables the extension to inject its content script into Google Form pages to analyze form structure and extract field values.
  • storage: Used to temporarily store multi-page form data within your browser's local storage as you navigate through a multi-page Google Form. This data is cleared once a pre-filled URL is generated or the form session ends.

Host Permissions

https://docs.google.com/forms/*

This permission is required to allow the extension's content script to run on Google Form pages. This is fundamental for the extension's core functionality: detecting Google Forms, analyzing their structure, extracting form field data, and generating pre-filled URLs. Access is limited strictly to Google Forms domains.

Data Processing

  • Form Analysis: The extension reads form field names and values from Google Forms
  • URL Generation: Creates pre-filled URLs using the form data
  • Multi-page Support: Temporarily stores data across form pages for complete URL generation
  • Local Storage: Uses Chrome's local storage API for temporary data retention

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking tools, or external APIs. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of collecting children's information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
